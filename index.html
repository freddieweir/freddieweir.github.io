About Fredrick's Motivations

Born on the small island of Jamaica as a "born abroad" citizen of the United States, Fredrick is a senior at Louisiana State University majoring in Information Systems and Decisions Sciences. After living for a brief time in Dalian, China as a teenager for his father's work, Fredrick came to understand just how much he took living in the United States for granted. Since then, there is nothing more Fredrick would want in life than to serve his country and give back to the country he loves, both in the military and civilian sector.
